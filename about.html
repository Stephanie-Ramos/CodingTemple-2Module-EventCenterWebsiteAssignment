About Our Venue

Grand Hall is built on service, creativity, and community-centered events.

Our History

Founded in 1998, Grand Hall transformed a restored city landmark into a multi-purpose event destination. Since opening, we have hosted more than 2,000 weddings, cultural festivals, and corporate gatherings.

Our spaces are designed to adapt to both formal and casual experiences while preserving architectural details from the original building.

Mission & Values

We create welcoming events that connect people through celebration, learning, and shared moments.

  • Hospitality first in every guest interaction
  • Inclusive event design and accessibility
  • Reliable planning and transparent communication
